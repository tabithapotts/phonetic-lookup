How to use this tool

Type any word or name into the box and press Look up. You'll see:

  • The IPA spelling — the standard phonetic alphabet used by linguists and voice coaches, e.g. /ˈlɑː.ɹə/
  • A plain-English guide — a simple breakdown like LAH-ruh, where capital letters show the stressed syllable
  • A play button — click Play to hear the word spoken in British English straight away

Where pronunciations come from: The tool searches the Free Dictionary API and Wiktionary, both of which use carefully checked Received Pronunciation (RP) entries. If a word isn't found, it offers direct links to Cambridge Dictionary, howjsay.com, and Forvo — all of which have British English recordings.
